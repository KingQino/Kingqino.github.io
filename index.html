Research Interests

My main research interest is mainly about Evolutionary Computation (EC), specifically:

  • Large-scale Global Optimization (LSGO) ALgorithms and Applications
  • Combinatorial Optimization Algorithms and Applications
  • Reinforcement Learning Algorithms and Applications
  • Multi-agent Systems
